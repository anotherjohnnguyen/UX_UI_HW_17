Who's John?
I’m a UX/UI designer based in San Francisco. Growing up in the Bay Area has taught me how appreciate this mixing pot of different cultures and personalities, and apply them into my works. As a designer, I value every part of the design process, from research to wire framing and design, because they are all essential parts of meeting the user end goals.

Other than the fact that I’m a big fan of circles, I also have a great passion for food and travel. When I’m not at my desk designing, you can find me at a hipster café sipping on a matcha latte, and possibly half way around the world. These are just ways for me to stay inspired and creative. Don’t be afraid to send me a message!
